About

I'm an 11th-grade student at Palo Alto High School with a strong interest in the safe and responsible use of artificial intelligence in medicine. My research focuses on how AI models can introduce bias in emergency room triage and how we can design and implement AI systems safely to improve accuracy without introducing bias. I hope to apply what I learn in real-world emergency room settings and also teach younger students about the safe and responsible use of AI.
